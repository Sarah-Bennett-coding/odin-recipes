Bacon

The Exemplary Side Protein

Bacon, the complimentary side that will have your mouth watering the moment you smell it. Serve bacon alongside eggs and toast for a nice, well rounded breakfast, that will help get you going in the morning!

Ingredients:

  • Bag of Bacon
  • Skillet
  • Fork or Spatula
  • Paper Towels or Newspaper

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Place the skillet on the stove
  2. Take 4 pieces of bacon and spread them evenly along the inside of the skillet
  3. Turn your stove to medium
  4. Diligently watch the bacon, flipping it regularly to ensure that both sides are cooked evenly
  5. For a less crunchy piece of bacon, keep it on the skillet til the edges are a light brown, for more crunch keep it on til it's a medium brown
  6. Remove bacon from the skillet with fork or spatula, and place them on a plate covered with paper towels/newspaper to allow the grease to drain
  7. Allow the bacon to drain and cool for 2-4 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
