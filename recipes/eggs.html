Eggs

The Mainstay of Any Breakfast

Eggs, the core protein of any morning meal! Best served with a side of toast and bacon, we're sure you'll find our recipe for basic sunny-side-up eggs delectable.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs (white or brown)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pan
  • Cooking Spray
  • Spatula

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Place your pan on your stove
  2. Take the cooking spray and apply a light coating to the inside of your pan
  3. Grab the eggs, and gingerly tap one egg at a time on the rim of the pan until it cracks
  4. With your thumbs placed adjacent to the crack, apply pressure to the crack, causing the egg to open and release the yoke into the pan
  5. Repeat the process with the other egg
  6. After discarding the eggshells, turn the heat on your stove to low
  7. As the eggs cook, apply salt and pepper to preferred taste
  8. Let eggs simmer til the outer egg has turned a nice white, and the yolk yellow
  9. Using your spatula, carefully pick up one egg at a time and place them on a plate
  10. Enjoy!

Note: for a more runny yoke, remove the eggs when they're a darker orange. For a more firm yolk remove the eggs when the yolk turns golden yellow.
