Toast

A Scrumptious Addition to Any Breakfast

Toast a breakfast staple that has been accompanying other classics such as eggs and bacon for centuries. Today we will be teaching you how to make your own slices of buttery heaven in the comfort of your own home. Simple, and easy to follow, you too will be able to enjoy this recipe with friends and family for years to come.

Ingredients:

  • Loaf of Bread (White or Wheat)
  • Butter
  • Butter Knife
  • Toaster

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Retrieve desired amount of bread from your loaf of bread (we will be using two pieces in this recipe)
  2. Place bread in the open slots on the top of your toaster
  3. Using the knob on the bottom of your toaster, turn the dial to 2 (you can change the number for how crisp you want your toast 1=lightly toasted 3-4=very, very crisp)
  4. Pull the lever on the toaster down
  5. Wait for the toast to cook
  6. When the toast is done, the toaster will make a popping sound, and pop out from the slots on the top of the toaster
  7. Wait 10-15 seconds for the toast to cool, and retrieve it from the toaster and place it on a plate
  8. Grab the butter, and the butter knife and put them by the bread
  9. Dip your knife in the butter, until you have a generous amount of it on the tip
  10. Gingerly spread the butter on your toast, until you have reached your desired amount of buttery goodness
  11. Enjoy!
